About the Society

The UoA Legal Tech Society is a student-run group advancing legal technology and innovation. Our mission is to connect students, professionals and academics exploring how technology is transforming the practice and study of law.

Writing Team: 52 active student authors publish in Nomiki Bibliothiki Daily (90 000+ monthly readers) and will present at the 1st Student Conference on Legal Informatics, November 2025.
